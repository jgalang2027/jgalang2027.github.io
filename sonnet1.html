SONNET #1

PETRARCHAN

A Heartbeat’s Waltz

The wise proclaim,“Fools in haste seldom win”

Yet, the melodies within my soul declare

An emotion I cannot bare

A waltz of heartbeats, offbeat and achin’

In caution’s shade, a rose’s life must begin

Though in its sweet embrace, I choose to dare

To waltz this waltz, a burden I am bound to bare

As love unfolds, a puzzle within

Through this burden, the rose begins to bloom

For our love weaves a masterpiece

A solution hidden in the sweetest harmony

Take my hand, as we walk through the path with peace

Through thick and thin, love will surely loom

The symphony of our hearts, an everlasting melody

SHAKESPEAREAN

Whispers of Devotion

In our few stolen moments, in shadows we confide

Others often claim your presence

I strive for a fate where I’m the only one desired

Yet, I find myself here, lying in his absence

Alone I dwell, in the solitude of my home

Advice from loved ones to seek a love anew

Countless attempts to once again find love’s promise

Attempts that result in tears that paint my skies so blue

You promised once, a life of sweet escape

Love grants the freedom we both yearned to share

Yet patience wanes, illusions start to drape

An olden fantasy, now worn and threadbare

But in the night, a revelation true

I save my love, only for you
