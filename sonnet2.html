SONNET #2

PETRARCHAN

Once Upon a Timeless Love

The celestial vast surrenders to your presence, as the glorious constellations yield to your alluring being.

No matter ceased to eclipse the luminous threads that weave the curves and smooths of which you are adorned.

My darling, casted splendor eludes as my gaze beholds a spectacle I adored.

Let us bask in the tranquil interlude as my world awakens in a symphony of golden hues beaming.

Fathom a delicate petal cradled in my hands that I yearn to show what truth is in the four letters of love yearning.

Entrance into the echoing rhythm that my heart bears as unfurling hearts collide at every beat clamored.

Velvet whispers’ first blessing with tenderness intertwined, heaven’s moonlit skies were left glamoured.

Surreal sensation encased in a timescape just like eternal classic love lingering.

Nestling in my depth lies a sanctuary to dispel the worries you bear.

Fairy tales lost their pixie dusts for you are the only wish that my heart ever made.

When there is no more weight in my steps, walk with me through the paradise of misty pave.

To my love that I shall hold and cherish ‘til the sun retreats to its shadows, my comfort I am willing to tear.

Just to embrace the moment we get to fleet beyond Infinity’s rave,

In old love’s story-tale.

SHAKESPEAREAN

The Single heartbeat

Only in the days of yore shall I know that it is within the hems of the breeze linger the bitter-sweet aroma of my father’s shattered heart

Soft-fleshed visage flustered with iridescent glistening crystalline echoes of the soul rush

Ever-moving cascading sand grappled between my mother’s fingers carries her wounded heart

Thou, melodies of love’s essence shall I only resonate lest defying the symphony of heart’s lush

Echoes of my spirits, concealed beneath the veneer of my skin, whisper that love is at the shallow crevice of the hands of time

Mere aura plastered across my solitaire demeanor, I refuse to unravel

Facaded solace confines my vulnerability from the pangs of love’s crime

‘Til the nocturnal viel blinds the dew-kissed heavens, I shall carry loneliness in my travel

I've forever stood as an unfazed soldier, a herald of paragons

But a scenery of sparked hope haunted my demons, how am I not unphased?

I've envisioned that in tomorrow’s glory, my embrace cradles hollow solace, an absent space that brings forth melancholic canons.

Assure me that my heart’s epiphany was not within the blur in the figment of my naive mind’s days.

Every stride is a testament to my faith in a world where uncertainties are certainly true.

Cause darling, if embers claim my last breath, my heart will endure, remember that life began when it beat solely for you.
